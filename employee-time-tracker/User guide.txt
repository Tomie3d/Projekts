Employee Time Tracking System - User Guide

1. Introduction

This guide explains how to use the Employee Time Tracking System. This system allows you to easily track your work hours.

2. Getting Started

Accessing the System: Open a web browser and navigate to the application's URL (provided by your administrator). You should see the landing page.

Registration:

Click the "Get Started" button on the landing page, which will take you to the login page.

Click the "Register here" link on the login page.

Fill in the registration form with your desired username, a strong password, and confirm the password.

Click the "Register" button.

You will be redirected to the login page and see a message indicating that your registration was successful. Important: You will need to wait for an administrator to approve your account before you can log in.

Logging In:

Go to the login page (you can reach it from the landing page by clicking "Get Started").

Enter your registered username and password.

Click the "Login" button.

If your credentials are correct and your account has been approved by an administrator, you will be redirected to the dashboard (or the admin panel if you are an administrator).

If you see an error message, double-check your username and password. If you still can't log in, your account might not be approved yet.

3. Using the Dashboard (Regular Users)

Clocking In:

After logging in, you'll be on the dashboard.

Click the green "Clock In" button to start recording your work time.

You should see a success message.

Clocking Out:

On the dashboard, click the red "Clock Out" button to stop recording your work time.

You should see a success message.

Viewing News:

The "Latest News" section on the dashboard displays news articles published by your administrator.

Logging Out:

Click the "Logout" button to securely exit the system.

Settings Panel:

Click the "Settings" button to open the settings panel.

Change Password:

Enter your current password.

Enter your desired new password.

Confirm your new password by entering it again.

Click "Change Password".

You'll see a success or error message.

Dark Mode: You can switch between light mode and dark mode using the "Dark Mode" switch in the settings panel.

4. Using the Admin Panel (Administrators)

Accessing the Admin Panel: Log in with an administrator account. You will be automatically redirected to the admin panel.

Managing Users:

User List: The "Registered Users" section shows a list of all users.

Approve/Reject: For pending users (marked as "Pending Approval"), you can click the "Approve" or "Reject" links to approve or reject their registration. Rejected users are deleted.

Grant/Revoke Admin: Click "Grant Admin" to give a user administrator privileges. Click "Revoke Admin" to remove administrator privileges. You cannot revoke your own admin status.

Delete User: Click "Delete User" to permanently remove a user account. You cannot delete your own account.

Viewing Time Logs:

The "Employee Time Logs" section displays a table of all recorded clock-in and clock-out times. The table includes:

Log ID: A unique identifier for each log entry.

Username: The username of the employee.

Clock In: The date and time the employee clocked in.

Clock Out: The date and time the employee clocked out (or "N/A" if they are still clocked in).

Resetting Logs:

Click the "Reset Logs" button to permanently delete all time log entries. This action cannot be undone.

Publishing News:

Publish News Form: Use the "Publish News" form to create news articles.

News Title: Enter a title for the news article.

News Content: Enter the content of the news article.

Click the "Publish" button to post the article.

All Published News: This is where all news articles are displayed

Edit: You can press the edit button in the dropdown and edit the specific news

Delete: You can press the delete button in the dropdown and delete the specific news

Logging Out:

Click the "Logout" button to securely exit the admin panel.

Settings Panel:

Click the "Settings" button to open the settings panel.

Change Password:

Enter your current password.

Enter your desired new password.

Confirm your new password by entering it again.

Click "Change Password".

You'll see a success or error message.

Dark Mode: You can switch between light mode and dark mode using the "Dark Mode" switch in the settings panel.
